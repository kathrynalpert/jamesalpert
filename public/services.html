About My Practice:

Currently psychiatrists tend to be viewed using medications as their primary treatment. Medications are tools psychiatrists may use but treating the whole person requires a psychiatrist to utilize one or more of the psychotherapies. A sound understanding of the history, fundamentals and current practice of the psychotherapies along with the experience and expertise to integrate them with them with medication, if necessary, is vital.

I have no office staff. I return consultation requests from about noon until 1 p.m. and at about 7 p.m. This ensures personal and confidential treatment. You are always speaking directly with me, not staff or paraprofessionals.
